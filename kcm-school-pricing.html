What is Keys, Codes & Modes?

Keys, Codes & Modes™ (KCM) is a browser-based visual music theory platform built around a proprietary 12-Color Chromatic Spectrum™ system. It gives students an intuitive, visual path into music theory — scales, chords, modes, intervals, and improvisation — through 25+ interactive apps that run on any device. No software to install. No hardware required. Works on Chromebooks, tablets, and desktops.
