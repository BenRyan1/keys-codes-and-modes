The Chromatic Circle shows all 12 musical notes arranged in a circle, with C at the top (12 o'clock position). This represents every possible note in Western music.

Musical Patterns are created by selecting specific notes from these 12. The pattern of intervals (Whole steps = W, Half steps = H) determines what we hear:
