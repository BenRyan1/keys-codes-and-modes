We stand behind our product. If you're not completely satisfied with Keys, Codes & Modes within 30 days of purchase, we'll refund your payment - no questions asked.

1. Our Refund Guarantee

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. That's why we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid subscriptions and the Founder's Circle membership.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

Eligible for Full Refund:

  • Requests made within 30 days of initial purchase
  • First-time purchasers of a specific plan
  • Technical issues preventing access (after support attempts)
  • Billing errors or duplicate charges

Not Eligible for Refund:

  • Requests made after the 30-day window
  • Violation of Terms of Service
  • Abuse of the refund policy (repeated purchase-refund cycles)
  • Pro-rated refunds for partial month usage (after 30 days)

5. Cancellation Policy

Monthly Subscriptions

  • Cancel anytime before your next billing date
  • Access continues until the end of your current billing period
  • No refund for unused time in current billing period (after 30-day window)
  • No automatic renewals after cancellation

Annual Subscriptions

  • Cancel anytime during the year
  • Access continues until the end of your annual period
  • Full refund if cancelled within 30 days of purchase
  • No pro-rated refund after 30-day window

Founder's Circle (Lifetime)

  • One-time payment - no recurring billing
  • Full refund if requested within 30 days
  • Cannot be cancelled after 30 days (lifetime access granted)

Downgrading

  • Changes take effect at the end of your current billing period

Upgrading

9. Special Circumstances

Technical Issues

If you experience technical issues preventing access to the Service:

We understand life happens. In cases of medical emergency or family hardship:
