Music Theory for Guitarists, Pianists & Composers

Whether you're a beginner trying to understand how music keys work, an intermediate guitarist learning the CAGED system and pentatonic scales, or an advanced player exploring modal theory and jazz harmony — Keys, Codes & Modes meets you where you are.

Our interactive music theory apps cover the circle of fifths, all seven musical modes (Ionian through Locrian), chord progressions, fretboard visualization, ear training, rhythm patterns, sacred geometry, and cymatics — turning abstract theory into something you can see, hear, and feel.
