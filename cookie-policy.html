1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the site owners.

Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies:

  • Persistent cookies remain on your device after you close your browser and allow the website to recognize your device on subsequent visits.
  • Session cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser.

2. How We Use Cookies

2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation, access to secure areas, and remembering your cookie consent preferences.

Note: These cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for the Site to work.

2.2 Analytics and Performance Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our Site. This helps us improve the user experience and optimize our content. We use Google Analytics with IP anonymization enabled to collect this information.

Data collected includes:

2.3 Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your preferred settings, scale selections, or playback preferences) to provide enhanced, personalized features.
