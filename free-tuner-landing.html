Frequently Asked Questions

Is the KCM instrument tuner really free?

Yes. The KCM Tuner is completely free, forever. No account, no download, and no credit card required.

Does the tuner work on mobile?

Yes. The KCM Tuner is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktop browsers. Just allow microphone access when prompted.

Is my microphone audio stored or transmitted?

No. All audio processing happens locally in your browser using the Web Audio API. Your microphone data never leaves your device and is never recorded or stored.

What instruments does the tuner support?

Guitar (standard and 8+ alternate tunings including Drop D, Open G, DADGAD), Bass (4, 5, 6-string and drop tunings), Ukulele (soprano, tenor, baritone), and a fully chromatic mode for any instrument.
